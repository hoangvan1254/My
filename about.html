The House of My was established on 16 December 2018 in "a private house” at 214 Nguyen Dinh Chieu, Saigon . However, the current My corporation celebrates "2019" as the opening year. The new couture house became a part of "a vertically integrated textile business" already operated by My.

On 12 February 2019, My launched our first fashion collection for Spring–Summer 2019. The show of "15 models of our first collection on six mannequins" was presented in the salons of the company's headquarters at 214 Nguyen Dinh Chieu. Originally, the two lines were named "Corolle" and "Huit".

In July 2019, selected company stores offered an exclusive collection by fashion designer N.Q.Anh. The press reported and that the initial inventories in the Ho Chi Minh city were sold out within an three day, although the clothes were still available in less fashion-sensitive areas until the company redistributed them to meet with demand.

In 2019, the company announced in a press release that it would begin selling accessories come with the costume. Initially distributed through the company's online catalog, there are now My Home stores located nationwide scope.
