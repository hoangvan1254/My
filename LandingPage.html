A New Way Of Style

As a long-term project of My, TOKYO DRIFT is a collection that uses Plaid motifs as the theme. Originally intended to create punk designs (one of the main lines of Japanese street fashion in general or Harajuku in particular), there are many different types of plaid, though. , we chose the most popular checkerboard with this fashion line - plaid tartan stripes in three basic colors of black, red and white.
